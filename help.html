Help Center

Navigating the Website

Use the navigation bar at the top of each page to move between different sections of the website:

  • Home: Main page with news and updates
  • Gallery & Stats: View images and player statistics
  • Stadium Tours: Book a tour of Stamford Bridge
  • Help: This page with usage instructions
  • About: Information about the project

Using the Gallery

The gallery page displays images of Stamford Bridge, the team, and trophies. Below the images you'll find:

  • A table with current squad information
  • Links to external Chelsea FC resources

Booking a Stadium Tour

To book a tour:

  1. Go to the Stadium Tours page
  2. Fill in all required fields (marked with *)
  3. Select your preferred tour type and date
  4. Indicate any special requirements
  5. Agree to the terms and conditions
  6. Click "Book Now" to submit your request

You'll receive a confirmation email once your booking is processed.

Viewing Player Statistics

The data table on the Gallery page shows:

  • Player squad numbers
  • Full names
  • Playing positions
  • Nationalities

The table footer shows when the data was last updated.
